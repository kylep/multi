## AI Industry

- [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/06/13/anthropic-disables-fable-mythos-export-controls-national-security-threat/) The US Commerce Department issued an export control directive on June 12 ordering Anthropic to disable Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 for all users — including foreign nationals employed by Anthropic. The government cited a jailbreak technique reportedly used by a Chinese group; Anthropic disputed the severity and is meeting with the Trump administration over the directive.

- [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/anthropic-files-confidential-1-joins-161008569.html) Anthropic filed a confidential draft S-1 with the SEC on June 1, formally beginning its IPO process. The company was last valued at approximately $965 billion following a $65 billion Series H round, with a reported revenue run-rate of $47 billion.

- [NVIDIA Newsroom](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/meta-builds-ai-infrastructure-with-nvidia) Meta and NVIDIA announced a multiyear strategic infrastructure partnership covering on-premises and cloud deployments, with Meta set to deploy millions of Blackwell and Rubin GPUs alongside expanded NVIDIA CPU and networking deployments.

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/02/nvidias-new-pc-chips-are-ceos-bid-to-own-every-part-of-ai-stack.html) NVIDIA unveiled RTX Spark, a laptop chip built in partnership with MediaTek, targeting Windows AI PCs. Launch partners include Microsoft, Dell, HP, ASUS, Lenovo, and MSI, with availability later in 2026.

## AI Tooling

- [Microsoft 365 Blog](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/blog/2026/06/16/copilot-cowork-is-now-generally-available/) Microsoft Copilot Cowork reached general availability on June 16, enabling AI-powered automation for complex enterprise tasks in Microsoft 365. Billing begins immediately; customers in the Frontier program have a grace period until July 1.

- [Sumato Solutions](https://sumatosolutions.com/blog-google-ai-updates-2026-gemini-flash-agentic-app-builder/) The original Gemini CLI reaches end-of-life today, June 18. Teams running automation or CI/CD pipelines against the old CLI must have migrated to the Agentic 2.0 CLI by this date.

- [spacedaily.com](https://spacedaily.com/n-microsoft-is-canceling-claude-code-licenses-across-its-experiences-devices-division-by-june-30-steering-thousands-of-engineers-toward-github-copilot-while-uber-burned-through-its-entire-2026-ai-bu/) Microsoft is cancelling Claude Code licenses for its Experiences and Devices division by June 30, redirecting engineers to GitHub Copilot CLI. Separately, Uber confirmed it exhausted its entire 2026 AI tools budget by April after rolling out Claude Code and Cursor to its engineering organization.

- [Lushbinary](https://lushbinary.com/blog/ai-coding-agents-comparison-cursor-windsurf-claude-copilot-kiro-2026/) Cursor restructured its Teams pricing in June: Standard seats at $32/seat/month (annual) and Premium seats at $96/seat/month (annual), replacing the prior single-tier model.

## Open Source

- [SecurityOnline](https://securityonline.info/cloudnativepg-vulnerability-cve-2026-44477-postgresql-rce/) CloudNativePG 1.29.1 and 1.28.3 were released to address CVE-2026-44477, a Critical (CVSS 9.4) privilege escalation flaw in the metrics exporter that could allow a low-privileged database user to gain PostgreSQL superuser access and execute arbitrary OS commands. All users should upgrade immediately.

- [GitHub](https://github.com/NVIDIA/SkillSpector) NVIDIA open-sourced SkillSpector, a security scanner for AI agent skills that detects vulnerabilities, malicious patterns, and injection risks. The project reached 7,700 stars and is trending this week.

## Security

- [Help Net Security](https://www.helpnetsecurity.com/2026/06/05/cisco-sd-wan-cve-2026-20245-0-day-exploited/) CVE-2026-20245, a zero-day in Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Manager, allows an authenticated attacker with netadmin privileges to execute arbitrary commands as root. Active exploitation has been observed; Cisco shipped a patch on June 10. This is the seventh SD-WAN zero-day Cisco has disclosed in 2026.

- [Tenable](https://www.tenable.com/blog/microsofts-june-2026-patch-tuesday-addresses-198-cves-cve-2026-49160-cve-2026-50507) Microsoft's June 2026 Patch Tuesday addressed a record 206 CVEs, including three publicly disclosed zero-days and 37 Critical vulnerabilities. CVE-2026-45657 (CVSS 9.8) is a Windows kernel RCE requiring no authentication or user interaction.
