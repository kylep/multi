## AI Industry

- [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/confidential-draft-s1-sec) Anthropic submitted a confidential draft S-1 to the SEC on June 1, targeting a valuation near $965 billion. Annualized revenue reached $44 billion in May 2026, up from $9 billion at end of 2025, with enterprise customers accounting for roughly 80% of revenue.

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/08/following-anthropic-openai-files-confidentially-for-ipo/) OpenAI filed its own confidential S-1 on June 8, one week after Anthropic. The company was last valued at $852 billion and cautioned that a public listing may still be some time away.

- [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-12/anthropic-openai-google-executives-plan-to-attend-g7-summit) Sam Altman, Dario Amodei, and Demis Hassabis are attending the G7 summit in France this week, the first time AI company executives have been formally included in G7 proceedings.

## AI Tooling

- [OpenTools](https://opentools.ai/news/microsoft-cancels-claude-code-licenses-copilot-cli) Microsoft is canceling Claude Code licenses for its Experiences + Devices division by June 30, directing engineers to GitHub Copilot CLI. Internal surveys showed engineers consistently preferred Claude Code due to a perceived feature gap in Copilot CLI.

- [Claude Code Docs](https://code.claude.com/docs/en/changelog) Claude Code v2.1.172 (June 10) adds nested sub-agents up to 5 levels deep, smarter model and region selection, a plugin search interface, and improved Chrome, VSCode, and terminal workflows.

## Security

- [Tenable](https://www.tenable.com/blog/microsofts-june-2026-patch-tuesday-addresses-198-cves-cve-2026-49160-cve-2026-50507) Microsoft patched 198 CVEs in June Patch Tuesday, the largest single-month release since the program began, with 32 rated critical. CVE-2026-45657 (Windows Kernel RCE, CVSS 9.8) and CVE-2026-47291 (HTTP.sys unauthenticated RCE, CVSS 9.8) are the highest-severity items; no exploitation confirmed yet.

## Geopolitics

- [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-12/anthropic-openai-google-executives-plan-to-attend-g7-summit) G7 leaders are meeting in France this week; AI governance is on the formal agenda for the first time, with tech executives invited as participants rather than observers.

## Local
