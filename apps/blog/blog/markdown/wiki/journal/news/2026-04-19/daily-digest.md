## AI Industry

- [Anthropic Blog / SaaStr](https://www.saastr.com/anthropic-just-passed-openai-in-revenue-while-spending-4x-less-to-train-their-models/) Anthropic reached $30B ARR in April 2026, surpassing OpenAI at $25B for the first time. Growth was 3.3x in four months from $9B at end of 2025; 80% of revenue comes from enterprise customers, with over 1,000 accounts each spending more than $1M annually.

- [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/tech/security/anthropic-project-glasswing-mythos-preview-claude-gets-limited-release-rcna267234) Anthropic released Claude Mythos Preview on April 8 to over 50 technology organizations through Project Glasswing, providing $100M+ in usage credits. The model autonomously identified and exploited a 17-year-old remote code execution vulnerability in FreeBSD and can find zero-days in major operating systems and browsers. Public release is withheld due to dual-use risk.

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/16/anthropic-claude-opus-4-7-model-mythos.html) Anthropic released Claude Opus 4.7 publicly on April 16, a model with deliberately reduced cyber capabilities compared to Mythos Preview. Security professionals seeking full capability access must apply through a formal verification program.

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/openai-launches-gpt-54-cyber-with.html) OpenAI released GPT-5.4-Cyber on April 14, a restricted model for security teams with expanded vulnerability research capabilities. Access is controlled via application, mirroring Anthropic's Glasswing model.

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/amp/2026/04/16/anthropic-london-office-800-staff-openai-expansion.html) Anthropic announced plans to expand its London presence to office space for 800 people, days after OpenAI announced its first permanent London office. Anthropic currently employs more than 200 people in the UK.

- [Quiver Quantitative](https://www.quiverquant.com/news/Google+in+Talks+With+Pentagon+to+Deploy+Gemini+AI+in+Classified+Settings+Amid+AI+Contract+Shifts) Alphabet is in discussions with the U.S. Department of Defense to deploy Gemini AI models in classified environments, according to reporting from April 16. The talks come as several major AI contracts are under review.

- [CoreWeave](https://www.coreweave.com/news/coreweave-and-meta-announce-21-billion-expanded-ai-infrastructure-agreement) CoreWeave and Meta announced an expanded cloud capacity agreement on April 9, valued at approximately $21 billion and running through December 2032. The deal covers AI training and inference workloads.

## AI Tooling

- [Android Developers Blog](https://android-developers.googleblog.com/2026/04/AI-Core-Developer-Preview.html) Google released Gemma 4, including gemma-4-26b-a4b-it and gemma-4-31b-it, available via AI Studio and the Gemini API. Gemma 4 serves as the foundation for the next generation of Gemini Nano; code written for Gemma 4 will automatically work on Gemini Nano 4 devices.

- [9to5Google](https://9to5google.com/2026/04/14/gemini-personal-intelligence-global/) Google began rolling out Personal Intelligence in the Gemini app globally (excluding Europe) on April 14. It is available first to AI Plus, Pro, and Ultra subscribers, with free-tier users to follow over the next few weeks.

- [The Register](https://www.theregister.com/2026/04/13/cloudflare_expanding_wrangler_cli_functionality/) Cloudflare rebuilt its Wrangler CLI on April 13 with broader API coverage, alongside a new unified "cf" CLI designed to give developers and AI agents consistent access to nearly 3,000 API operations. The Cloudflare AI Gateway now supports 70+ models across 12+ providers through a single endpoint.

- [Microsoft Copilot Blog](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-copilot/blog/copilot-studio/new-and-improved-multi-agent-orchestration-connected-experiences-and-faster-prompt-iteration/) Microsoft Copilot Studio multi-agent orchestration became generally available in April 2026, adding cross-Fabric coordination and open Agent-to-Agent (A2A) protocol support so agents can collaborate across ecosystems.

- [Havoptic](https://www.havoptic.com/tools/github-copilot) GitHub Copilot CLI shipped multiple updates between April 10 and 16, including improvements to MCP server installation, permission prompts, and agentic workflow support.

## Open Source

- [GitHub](https://github.com/ggml-org/llama.cpp/releases) llama.cpp shipped four tagged releases between April 12 and 13, with the headline addition being a Vulkan flash attention DP4A shader for quantized KV cache. The change extends performance improvements to non-NVIDIA GPU hardware.

- [GitHub](https://github.com/NousResearch/hermes-agent) NousResearch/hermes-agent accumulated 42,612 stars this week, reaching 100,533 total. The project describes itself as an agent framework designed to grow with usage.

## Security

- [Cybersecurity News](https://cybersecuritynews.com/microsoft-patch-tuesday-april-2026/) Microsoft's April 2026 Patch Tuesday addressed 168 CVEs. CVE-2026-33825, a Windows Defender zero-day actively being exploited, allows an unprivileged local user to escalate to SYSTEM on fully patched Windows 10 and 11. CVE-2026-33824 is a critical unauthenticated RCE in Windows IKE Service Extensions with a CVSS score of 9.8.

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/adobe-patches-actively-exploited.html) Adobe patched CVE-2026-34621, an actively exploited zero-day in Acrobat Reader that runs malicious JavaScript when a specially crafted PDF is opened. Evidence suggests the vulnerability was under exploitation as early as December 2025.
