## AI Industry

- [The Information via Reuters](https://www.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/chinas-deepseek-is-raising-funds-at-10-billion-valuation-the-information-reports-4621119) DeepSeek is seeking its first-ever external funding, targeting $300M at a valuation above $10B. The company previously relied entirely on its parent, high-frequency trading firm High-Flyer Capital Management; rising training costs for its V4 model and a 12-hour outage in March are cited as drivers.

## AI Tooling

- [Releasebot](https://releasebot.io/updates/anthropic/claude-code) Claude Code v2.1.111 (April 17) adds Opus 4.7 xhigh model tier accessible via the `/effort` command, letting users dial speed vs. intelligence. Auto mode for Max subscribers defaults to Opus 4.7. Also adds PowerShell tool on Windows, Ctrl+U input-clear shortcut, and a terminal appearance sync option.

## Geopolitics

- [CNN](https://edition.cnn.com/2026/04/18/world/live-news/iran-war-trump-israel) Iran re-closed the Strait of Hormuz on April 18 (Day 50 of the conflict), reversing the brief reopening from April 17. The two-week ceasefire expires April 21 with no deal on nuclear enrichment, war damages, or Hormuz terms. Trump threatened to bomb Iranian power plants and bridges if talks collapse; Iran's chief negotiator said passage cannot be allowed while Iranian ports remain blockaded.

## Local

## Just for You

- [GitHub Security Advisory](https://github.com/advisories/GHSA-v38x-c887-992f) A remote code execution vulnerability was disclosed April 18 in Flowise, the popular open-source AI agent and workflow builder. The Airtable Agent component is vulnerable to code injection via unsanitized user input. No patch version cited in initial disclosure; Flowise users should check the advisory and monitor for an update.
