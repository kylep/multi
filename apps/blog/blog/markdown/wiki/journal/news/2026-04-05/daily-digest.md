## Security

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/new-chrome-zero-day-cve-2026-5281-under.html) CVE-2026-5281: actively exploited use-after-free in Chrome's Dawn (WebGPU) engine, marking the fourth Chrome zero-day patched in 2026. Fixed in Chrome 146.0.7680.177/178 on Windows/macOS and 146.0.7680.177 on Linux. All Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Opera, Vivaldi) are also affected. CISA added it to KEV; federal agencies must patch by April 15.
