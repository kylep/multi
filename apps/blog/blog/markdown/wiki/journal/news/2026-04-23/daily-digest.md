## AI Industry

- [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/04/23/anthropic-openai-showdown) Anthropic is managing multiple simultaneous problems as it approaches a potential IPO at an estimated $800B valuation: a software update accidentally exposed Claude Code internal files, the company is investigating unauthorized access to its restricted Mythos model, and a limited test removing Claude Code from the $20/mo Pro plan drew public backlash. Revenue is reported at $30B ARR.

- [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/04/23/anthropic-openai-showdown) A leaked memo from OpenAI chief revenue officer Denise Dresser called Anthropic "elitist" and accused the company of overstating its revenue run rate by billions. CEO Sam Altman separately called Anthropic's marketing approach "fear-based" in a podcast appearance this week.

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/anthropic-ai-europe-data-center-capacity-role.html) Anthropic is hiring a six-figure role to negotiate data center capacity deals in Europe, signaling a push to expand AI infrastructure outside the US.

## Security

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/21/unauthorized-group-has-gained-access-to-anthropics-exclusive-cyber-tool-mythos-report-claims/) Anthropic is investigating unauthorized access to Claude Mythos Preview, a model restricted from public release due to its ability to find and exploit software vulnerabilities at near-superhuman capability. A contractor's credentials, combined with data from a breach at AI recruiting firm Mercor, were used to reach the model. Mythos Preview had been shared with roughly 50 organizations including Apple, Amazon, Cisco, CrowdStrike, Google, JPMorgan, Microsoft, and NVIDIA. Anthropic says no activity was detected outside the vendor environment.

## Geopolitics

- [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/04/22/nx-s1-5795405/iran-middle-east-updates) Iran confirmed it will not reopen the Strait of Hormuz while the US naval blockade continues, calling the blockade an act of war. On April 23 Iran seized additional commercial vessels transiting the strait. Trump extended the ceasefire indefinitely pending a unified Iranian proposal, but no new talks are scheduled.
