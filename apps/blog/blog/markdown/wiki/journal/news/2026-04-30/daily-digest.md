## AI Industry

- [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/04/30/openai-anthropic-winners-losers-ipo) Anthropic is fielding unsolicited offers for a new $50B round at an $850–900B valuation, with a board decision expected in May. The company hit a $40B annualized revenue run rate, reaching its year-end target eight months early.

- [AWS / OpenAI](https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/2026/04/bedrock-openai-models-codex-managed-agents/) One day after the Microsoft exclusivity deal dissolved, AWS added GPT-5.5, GPT-5.4, and Codex to Amazon Bedrock in limited preview, plus a new Bedrock Managed Agents service powered by OpenAI.

- [Google / Alphabet](https://theaiinsider.tech/2026/04/30/google-bets-big-on-ai-across-tv-photos-and-cloud-as-gemini-powers-record-growth/) Google Cloud posted its best quarter to date, exceeding $20B in revenue — a 63% year-over-year increase. Token usage through the Gemini API hit 16 billion per minute, up from 10 billion last quarter. A $462B unfilled order backlog signals sustained demand.

- [Axios / Meta](https://www.axios.com/2026/04/23/meta-layoffs-ai-efficiency-push) Meta confirmed it will cut 10% of its workforce — approximately 8,000 employees — starting May 20, while committing $115–135B in AI spending for 2026, including a multiyear partnership with NVIDIA for Blackwell and Rubin GPU deployments.

## AI Tooling

- [Microsoft / TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/29/microsoft-says-it-has-over-20m-paid-copilot-users-and-they-really-are-using-it/) Microsoft 365 Copilot reached 20 million paid enterprise seats, with the number of companies holding over 50,000 seats quadrupling. Agent mode — enabling multi-step autonomous actions in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint — is now the default Copilot experience.

## Open Source

- [Hugging Face / GitHub](https://github.com/huggingface/ml-intern) huggingface/ml-intern re-entered the weekly trending top 8 with 6,388 stars this week. The project is a fully autonomous ML engineer that reads arXiv papers, finds datasets, runs training, and iterates on eval without human input.

## Security

- [CISA / The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/cisa-adds-8-exploited-flaws-to-kev-sets.html) CISA added eight actively exploited flaws to its KEV catalog. Top priority: CVE-2025-32975 (CVSS 10.0) in Quest KACE Systems Management Appliance allowing unauthenticated user impersonation; three Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Manager flaws (CVE-2026-20122, 20128, 20133) with federal patch deadlines of April 23 and May 4.

## Geopolitics

- [YouTube / Geo News](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pEHujkL8o10) Trump postponed threatened strikes on Iranian energy infrastructure for five days pending the outcome of ongoing negotiations. Strait of Hormuz traffic remains at a fraction of pre-conflict levels and Brent crude stayed above $110/barrel.

- [IMF](https://www.imf.org/en/publications/weo/issues/2026/04/14/world-economic-outlook-april-2026) The IMF's April 2026 World Economic Outlook projects global growth slowing to 3.1% in 2026, with headline inflation rising modestly due to the ongoing Middle East conflict and energy market disruption.

## Local

- [CP24](https://www.cp24.com/local/toronto/2026/04/29/extended-runway-land-at-billy-bishop-could-be-more-than-two-kilometres-long/) The Toronto Port Authority outlined plans that could extend Billy Bishop Airport's runway by up to 900 metres — requiring new infill in Lake Ontario — to accommodate jets such as the Airbus A220. Premier Ford announced the province is taking over Toronto's seat on the airport governance board to advance the expansion; Mayor Chow called it a power grab.
