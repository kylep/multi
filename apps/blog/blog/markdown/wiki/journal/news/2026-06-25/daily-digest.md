## AI Industry

## Security

- [BleepingComputer](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/cisa-splunk-enterprise-flaw-actively-exploited-patch-by-sunday/) CVE-2026-20253 is a pre-authenticated RCE in Splunk Enterprise (versions 10.0.0–10.0.6 and 10.2.0–10.2.3) via an unauthenticated PostgreSQL sidecar service endpoint. Active exploitation confirmed; CISA added it to the Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog June 18 with a federal agency patch deadline of June 26. Fixed in 10.0.7 and 10.2.4.
