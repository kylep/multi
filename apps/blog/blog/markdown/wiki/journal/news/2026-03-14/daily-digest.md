**Policy & Regulation**
- The [US Commerce Department](https://www.commerce.gov) withdrew a draft rule on March 14 that would have required permits for global AI chip exports exceeding 200,000 units, with an official stating the rule "was always a draft." [(Bloomberg)](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-14/us-withdraws-draft-rule-that-called-for-global-ai-chip-permits)
- The [European Council](https://www.consilium.europa.eu) adopted a position on March 13 to amend the EU AI Act, adding an explicit ban on AI tools that generate non-consensual sexual imagery and easing compliance requirements for AI embedded in sector-regulated products such as medical devices. [(Council of the EU)](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/press/press-releases/2026/03/13/council-agrees-position-to-streamline-rules-on-artificial-intelligence/)
- Washington State lawmakers gave final passage to HB 2225, a bill requiring safety disclosures and guardrails for AI companion chatbot services. [(Transparency Coalition)](https://www.transparencycoalition.ai/news/ai-legislative-update-march13-2026)

**Industry**
- [Meta](https://meta.com) is reportedly considering layoffs affecting up to 20% of its workforce (approximately 16,000 employees) to offset surging AI infrastructure costs; the company called the report "speculative." [(TechCrunch)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/14/meta-reportedly-considering-layoffs-that-could-affect-20-of-the-company/)
- Meta has postponed the release of its next-generation AI model codenamed "Avocado" to at least May 2026 after internal benchmarks showed it underperforming rival models, and the company is in preliminary talks to license [Google](https://deepmind.google) Gemini as an interim solution. [(TopAIProduct)](https://topaiproduct.com/2026/03/14/only-2-of-11-co-founders-left-xai-hires-cursors-top-engineers-to-rebuild-its-coding-tool-from-scratch/)
- [xAI](https://x.ai) has hired Andrew Milich and Jason Ginsberg from [Cursor](https://cursor.com) to rebuild its AI coding division after 9 of its original 11 co-founders departed and internal efforts failed to compete with rivals. [(TechCrunch)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/13/not-built-right-the-first-time-musks-xai-is-starting-over-again-again/)
- [Anthropic](https://anthropic.com) launched the Claude Partner Network on March 12 with a $100 million initial commitment, enrolling Accenture, Deloitte, Cognizant, and Infosys as anchor partners; Accenture plans to train 30,000 professionals on Claude and Deloitte has opened Claude access to 350,000 associates. [(Anthropic)](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-partner-network)

**Model Releases**
- [NVIDIA](https://nvidia.com) released Nemotron 3 Super on March 11, a 120B-parameter hybrid Mamba-Transformer MoE model with 12B active parameters, a 1M-token context window, and up to 2.2× throughput improvement over comparable open models. [(NVIDIA Technical Blog)](https://developer.nvidia.com/blog/introducing-nemotron-3-super-an-open-hybrid-mamba-transformer-moe-for-agentic-reasoning/)
- [Hume AI](https://www.hume.ai) open-sourced TADA (Text-Acoustic Dual Alignment) on March 10 under the MIT license, a 1B/3B-parameter speech-language model that generates text and audio in one synchronized stream and produced zero hallucinations across 1,000+ test samples. [(Hume AI Blog)](https://www.hume.ai/blog/opensource-tada)

**Research**
- [Allen Institute for AI (Ai2)](https://allenai.org) released MolmoBot and MolmoSpaces on March 12, demonstrating zero-shot sim-to-real transfer for robot manipulation tasks trained entirely in simulation with no real-world data collection. [(Ai2)](https://allenai.org/blog/molmobot)
- Economists presenting at Stanford's SIEPR summit on March 14 reported that entry-level software developer hiring has declined 20% and call center positions are down 15%, attributing the drops to enterprise AI adoption. [(Creati.ai)](https://creati.ai/ai-news/2026-03-14/)

**Tools & Infrastructure**
- [Google](https://blog.google) launched "Ask Maps," a Gemini-powered conversational feature for Google Maps that handles complex natural-language location queries, alongside a redesigned Immersive Navigation with 3D visuals and real-time road details, rolling out in the US and India on March 12. [(Google Blog)](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/maps/ask-maps-immersive-navigation/)
- [NVIDIA](https://nvidia.com)'s GPU Technology Conference 2026 opens March 16 in San Jose, where CEO Jensen Huang is expected to unveil Rubin-generation GPUs, the Vera CPU, and the company's agentic AI platform. [(SiliconAngle)](https://siliconangle.com/2026/03/14/gtc-preview-inside-ai-factory-1t-infrastructure-war-hood-ai-economy/)
