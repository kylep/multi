## AI Industry

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/openai-slams-anthropic-in-memo-to-shareholders-as-rival-gains-momentum.html) OpenAI circulated an investor memo claiming it will reach 30 GW of compute by 2030 versus Anthropic's projected 7–8 GW by end of 2027. The memo was sent in response to Anthropic's Mythos model announcement and Anthropic's revenue crossing $30B ARR.

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/08/meta-debuts-the-muse-spark-model-in-a-ground-up-overhaul-of-its-ai/) Meta launched Muse Spark, the first model from Alexandr Wang's Superintelligence Labs. It is closed-source — breaking from Meta's Llama open-source tradition — accepts voice, text, and image input, and powers the Meta AI app and Meta.ai website immediately. Benchmarks show it competitive with but not surpassing leading models from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google.

- [CoreWeave](https://www.coreweave.com/news/coreweave-and-meta-announce-21-billion-expanded-ai-infrastructure-agreement) Meta and CoreWeave signed a $21B expanded agreement through December 2032 for access to Rubin-based GPU clusters, covering next-generation model training including Llama 5.

## AI Tooling

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/09/chatgpt-pro-plan-100-month-codex/) OpenAI added a $100/month ChatGPT Pro tier positioned between Plus ($20) and the existing Pro ($200). It includes 5x more Codex usage than Plus and full access to Instant and Thinking models. A promotional offer runs through May 31 with up to 10x Codex access.

- [TechBriefly](https://techbriefly.com/2026/04/10/google-fully-integrates-notebooklm-into-gemini/) Google fully integrated NotebookLM into the Gemini app, allowing notebook creation directly within the chatbot interface. The update also adds free Personal Intelligence that connects Gemini to Gmail and Drive, plus cross-platform chat import.

## Open Source

- [Google Blog](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/developers-tools/gemma-4/) Google released Gemma 4 on April 2 under Apache 2.0. The family covers four sizes (E2B, E4B, 26B MoE, and 31B Dense), all with multimodal input support. Larger models carry a 256K context window. Available on Hugging Face, Ollama, and Kaggle.

## Security

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/backdoored-smart-slider-3-pro-update.html) Attackers compromised Nextend's update servers and pushed a backdoored Smart Slider 3 Pro 3.5.1.35 to WordPress sites with 800k+ active installations. The malicious build was live for approximately six hours on April 7 before removal. The payload creates rogue admin accounts and installs multi-layered remote access tools. Users should update to 3.5.1.36 immediately.

## Geopolitics

- [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com) Only nine vessels transited the Strait of Hormuz in the 24 hours following the US–Iran ceasefire, against a normal daily volume of around 150. The IEA described the disruption as the most significant supply shock in the history of the oil market. Brent fell from $114 to around $95/barrel on ceasefire news.
