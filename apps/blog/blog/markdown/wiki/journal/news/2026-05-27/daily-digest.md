The repo entered GitHub weekly trending with 4,086 stars this week.

## Security

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/2026-year-of-ai-assisted-attacks.html) Microsoft identified an active cryptojacking campaign using AI chatbot interactions to surface malicious download sites. Attackers impersonate legitimate system utilities such as CrystalDiskInfo and HWMonitor to target users with high-performance GPUs, then establish persistent remote access beyond the initial mining payload.

## Local

- [CP24](https://www.cp24.com/politics/queens-park/2026/05/26/managers-will-be-able-to-let-ontario-civil-servants-work-from-home-for-2-days-of-world-cup/) Ontario's Ford government announced managers may approve temporary remote work for OPS staff on two daytime World Cup match days in Toronto: June 12 and June 26. The policy is more limited than the full June 8–July 3 flexibility the union had sought.
