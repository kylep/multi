## AI Industry

- [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/higher-limits-spacex) Anthropic signed a $1.25B/month compute agreement with SpaceX to lease all available capacity at the Colossus 1 data center near Memphis — over 220,000 NVIDIA GPUs and 300 MW. The deal runs through 2029 and was paired with doubled Claude Code usage limits for Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans and removal of peak-hour caps.

## AI Tooling

- [Cybernews](https://cybernews.com/ai-news/claude-outage-resolved-anthropic-opus-model-errors/) Anthropic is investigating unconfirmed claims that a roughly two-hour outage on Friday may have exposed customer data. The outage primarily affected Opus 4.7 and 4.8 models, and Anthropic said it resolved the disruption before opening the investigation.

- [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news) Anthropic released 20+ legal MCP connectors and 12 practice-area plugins for Claude, extending integrations across legal research, contracts, discovery, matter management, and legal aid workflows.

## Geopolitics

- [Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/my-europe/2026/06/08/nikol-pashinyan-hails-historic-victory-in-decisive-election-in-armenia) Armenian Prime Minister Nikol Pashinyan declared victory in Sunday's parliamentary election after his Civil Contract party took approximately 57% of the vote. ## AI Industry

- [Apple](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/) Apple kicked off WWDC 2026 today with its annual developer keynote, the last under Tim Cook as CEO. Announced software updates across all platforms — iOS 27, iPadOS 27, macOS 27, watchOS 27, tvOS 27, and visionOS 27 — headlined by a rebuilt Siri powered by a Gemini-based model, a standalone Siri app with chatbot interface, and an Extensions system letting users select ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude as the default AI for Apple Intelligence features.

## AI Tooling

- [Google Cloud](https://docs.cloud.google.com/gemini/enterprise/docs/release-notes) Starting June 8, Gemini 3.5 Flash is enabled by default in Gemini Enterprise and cannot be turned off. The model launched at Google I/O in late May as the first in the Gemini 3.5 generation combining frontier reasoning with action capabilities.

- [Microsoft](https://windowsnews.ai/article/microsoft-build-2026-ai-agents-copilot-azure-ai-foundry-and-windows-local-ai.420861) At Build 2026 (June 2-3), Microsoft shipped a preview of the GitHub Copilot desktop app bringing agentic workflows to a native client, announced WinUI AI agent tooling supporting both Copilot and Claude Code, and made Microsoft IQ generally available across Copilot Studio, GitHub Copilot, and Azure AI Foundry.

## Security

- [Bleeping Computer](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/new-cisco-sd-wan-flaw-exploited-in-zero-day-attacks-to-gain-root/) Cisco disclosed CVE-2026-20245, an actively exploited zero-day in Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Manager that allows authenticated local attackers to execute arbitrary commands as root via a crafted file upload. No patch is available. This is the seventh SD-WAN zero-day Cisco has disclosed in 2026; Mandiant reported the flaw after observing exploitation including unauthorized configuration pushes to edge devices.
