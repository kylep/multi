## AI Industry

- [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/higher-limits-spacex) Anthropic signed a $1.25B/month compute agreement with SpaceX to lease all available capacity at the Colossus 1 data center near Memphis — over 220,000 NVIDIA GPUs and 300 MW. The deal runs through 2029 and was paired with doubled Claude Code usage limits for Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans and removal of peak-hour caps.

## AI Tooling

- [Cybernews](https://cybernews.com/ai-news/claude-outage-resolved-anthropic-opus-model-errors/) Anthropic is investigating unconfirmed claims that a roughly two-hour outage on Friday may have exposed customer data. The outage primarily affected Opus 4.7 and 4.8 models, and Anthropic said it resolved the disruption before opening the investigation.

- [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news) Anthropic released 20+ legal MCP connectors and 12 practice-area plugins for Claude, extending integrations across legal research, contracts, discovery, matter management, and legal aid workflows.

## Geopolitics

- [Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/my-europe/2026/06/08/nikol-pashinyan-hails-historic-victory-in-decisive-election-in-armenia) Armenian Prime Minister Nikol Pashinyan declared victory in Sunday's parliamentary election after his Civil Contract party took approximately 57% of the vote. The pro-Russian Strong Armenia alliance came second at 21%. Pashinyan declared victory shortly after 2 a.m. June 8; OSCE preliminary findings are scheduled for June 8 at 15:00 local time.
