## AI Industry

- [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/economy/2026/5/6/spacex-backs-anthropic-with-data-centre-deal-amidst-musks-openai-lawsuit) Anthropic signed a compute deal with SpaceX to use Colossus 1, a Memphis facility housing over 220,000 Nvidia GPUs, adding 300 megawatts of capacity within a month.

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/04/anthropic-and-openai-are-both-launching-joint-ventures-for-enterprise-ai-services/) Anthropic and OpenAI both completed PE-backed joint ventures for enterprise AI consulting. Anthropic's entity is seeded with $1.5 billion (Blackstone and Hellman & Friedman as lead investors); OpenAI's entity is seeded at $4 billion on a $10 billion pre-money valuation.

- [Google DeepMind](https://deepmind.google/blog/alphaevolve-impact/) Google DeepMind expanded AlphaEvolve's scope, reporting the Gemini-powered coding agent has made new discoveries in open mathematics and CS problems, and optimized quantum circuits on the Willow processor to achieve 10x lower error rates than prior baselines.

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/06/nvidia-corning-optical-factories-nc-texas-ai.html) NVIDIA committed up to $3.2 billion to invest in Corning as part of a deal to build three optical fiber manufacturing plants in North Carolina and Texas, expanding U.S. AI data center supply chain capacity.

## Security

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/cisa-adds-actively-exploited-linux-root.html) CISA added CVE-2026-31431 (dubbed "Copy Fail") to its Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog. The Linux kernel local privilege escalation flaw allows an unprivileged user to obtain root access and affects Red Hat, SUSE, Ubuntu, and AWS Linux distributions.

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/palo-alto-pan-os-flaw-under-active.html) CISA added CVE-2026-0300 to the KEV catalog on May 6. The Palo Alto PAN-OS flaw carries a CVSS score of 9.3 and allows unauthenticated remote code execution when the User-ID Authentication Portal is internet-exposed.

- [DailyCVE](https://dailycve.com/argo-cd-authorization-bypass-cve-2026-43824-critical/) CVE-2026-43824, a critical authorization bypass in Argo CD, was disclosed May 7. The `ServerSideDiff` endpoint exposes Kubernetes Secret values to any authenticated user by default. Patched versions are 3.3.9 and 3.2.11.
