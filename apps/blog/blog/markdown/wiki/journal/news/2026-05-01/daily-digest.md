## AI Industry

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/24/google-to-invest-up-to-40b-in-anthropic-in-cash-and-compute/) Google committed to invest up to $40 billion in Anthropic in cash and compute, with $10 billion upfront at a $350 billion valuation and up to $30 billion more tied to performance targets.

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/07/anthropic-mythos-ai-model-preview-security/) Anthropic released Claude Mythos Preview in limited access via Project Glasswing, a consortium including Amazon, Apple, Cisco, CrowdStrike, Microsoft, and the Linux Foundation. The model is not publicly available; Anthropic cited cybersecurity capability concerns as the reason for restricted access.

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/16/anthropic-claude-opus-4-7-model-mythos.html) Anthropic also released Claude Opus 4.7 as its most capable generally available model, with improvements in software engineering, instruction-following, and real-world task completion.

- [The Register](https://www.theregister.com/2026/04/30/openai_anthropic_top_lines_research_counterpoint/) Anthropic topped OpenAI in global LLM revenue for Q1 2026 with a 31.4% share versus OpenAI's 29%, according to Counterpoint Research, driven by fewer but higher-paying enterprise customers.

- [Microsoft Blog](https://blogs.microsoft.com/blog/2026/04/21/accelerating-frontier-transformation-with-microsoft-partners/) Microsoft launched Microsoft 365 E7: The Frontier Suite and Agent 365 into general availability on May 1. Agent 365 is priced at $15 per user and adds autonomous task execution across email, calendar, and Microsoft 365 apps.

- [NVIDIA Newsroom](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/meta-builds-ai-infrastructure-with-nvidia) Meta and NVIDIA announced a multiyear strategic partnership deploying millions of Blackwell and Rubin GPUs plus NVIDIA Grace CPUs as standalone processors in Meta's data centers — the first large-scale standalone Grace CPU deployment.

## AI Tooling

- [Google Cloud Blog](https://cloud.google.com/blog/products/ai-machine-learning/claude-mythos-preview-on-vertex-ai) Claude Mythos Preview is available through Amazon Bedrock and Google Vertex AI for Project Glasswing partner organizations, extending Anthropic's enterprise distribution reach.

- [Yahoo Tech](https://tech.yahoo.com/ai/gemini/articles/gemini-now-generate-files-google-102500723.html) Google updated Gemini to generate downloadable files directly in the chat interface, including PDF, DOCX, XLSX, CSV, LaTeX, and Markdown formats.

- [Google Blog](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/infrastructure-and-cloud/google-cloud/next-2026/) Google Cloud Next 2026 announced the Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform — a workspace for building, governing, and deploying AI agents at scale using Gemini 3.1 Pro.

## Open Source

- [Kubernetes Blog](https://kubernetes.io/blog/2026/04/22/kubernetes-v1-36-release/) Kubernetes v1.36 "Haru" released with 70 enhancements: Dynamic Resource Allocation (DRA), In-Place Pod Resize, User Namespaces, and fine-grained Kubelet API Authorization all graduated to GA.

## Security

- [SecurityWeek](https://www.securityweek.com/critical-github-vulnerability-exposed-millions-of-repositories/) A critical RCE vulnerability (CVE-2026-3854) was disclosed in GitHub's internal Git infrastructure, allowing any authenticated user to execute arbitrary commands on backend servers. GitHub patched GitHub.com on March 4, 2026 and Enterprise Server on March 10, but as of late April 88% of Enterprise Server instances remained unpatched.

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/cisa-adds-4-exploited-flaws-to-kev-sets.html) CISA added four actively exploited vulnerabilities to its KEV catalog — including a CVSS 9.9 flaw in SimpleHelp (CVE-2024-57726) — with a federal remediation deadline of May 8, 2026.

## Geopolitics

- [European Commission](https://ec.europa.eu/commission/presscorner/detail/en/ip_26_184) The EU and India concluded a free trade agreement covering two billion people and roughly 25% of global GDP. India will reduce or eliminate tariffs on 96.6% of EU exports; the agreement requires parliamentary ratification and could enter force in early 2027.

- [One News Page](https://www.onenewspage.com/n/World/1ztf28oxr7/Red-Square-2026-Different-Victory-Day-Parade.htm) Russia's Victory Day military parade is scheduled for May 9 in Red Square, the first since escalating external pressure on the country's armed forces.
