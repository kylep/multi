## AI Industry

- [NVIDIA Newsroom](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-microsoft-windows-pcs-agents-rtx-spark) NVIDIA unveiled the RTX Spark superchip at Computex on June 1 — an Arm-based, 1-petaflop chip co-developed with MediaTek targeting the PC CPU market. Windows AI agent PCs from Microsoft Surface, Dell, HP, ASUS, Lenovo, and MSI are shipping this fall, each with secure per-agent sandboxes jointly developed with Microsoft and local LLM support.

## Geopolitics

- [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-03/why-strait-of-hormuz-blockade-is-at-heart-of-us-iran-peace-deal-stalemate) The US ended its naval blockade of Iranian ports May 29, but Strait of Hormuz shipping remains at roughly 5% of pre-conflict levels. Maersk, CMA CGM, and Hapag-Lloyd have suspended transits; Europe faces 4–6 months to normalise supply chains with the Red Sea route also restricted.

- [WEF](https://www.weforum.org/stories/2026/05/blockade-diplomacy-and-other-geopolitical-stories-to-know-this-month/) China unveiled 10 new measures to deepen ties with Taiwan, including resumption of direct flights to five additional mainland cities and easing of individual travel bans. The move follows a KMT opposition leader's visit to Beijing in April and precedes the expected Trump–Xi summit.
