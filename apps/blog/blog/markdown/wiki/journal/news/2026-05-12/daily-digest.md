## AI Industry

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/openai-launches-daybreak-for-ai-powered.html) OpenAI launched Daybreak on May 12, a cybersecurity platform built on GPT-5.5, GPT-5.5 Trusted Access, and GPT-5.5-Cyber. It performs vulnerability triage, patch generation, patch validation, and secure code review. Launch partners include Akamai, Cisco, Cloudflare, CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, and Zscaler.

- [TechStory](https://techstory.in/eu-commission-opens-strategic-dialogue-with-openai-and-anthropic/) The EU Commission confirmed on May 11 it opened formal strategic dialogue with OpenAI and Anthropic, seeking advance access to general-purpose AI models before EU AI Office enforcement powers activate in August 2026.

## AI Tooling

- [9to5Google](https://9to5google.com/2026/05/12/gemini-intelligence-announcement/) Google announced Gemini Intelligence at the Android Show: I/O Edition — an agentic layer baked into Android that can move across apps and complete multi-step tasks without user intervention. First rollout to Pixel and Samsung Galaxy devices this summer; watches, cars, and laptops to follow later in 2026.

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/12/google-unveils-googlebooks-a-new-line-of-ai-native-laptops/) Google unveiled Googlebook, a line of AI-native laptops built around Gemini Intelligence in partnership with Acer, ASUS, Dell, HP, and Lenovo. Ships this fall with "Magic Pointer," an AI cursor that surfaces Gemini suggestions based on screen context.

## Open Source

- [Red Hat Developer](https://developers.redhat.com/blog/2026/05/04/kafka-monthly-digest-april-2026) Strimzi 1.0.0, the Kubernetes operator for Apache Kafka, released. It drops all legacy CRD API versions in favor of v1 only, adds Kafka 4.1.2 support, HTTPS for the bridge component, and in-place pod resizing for brokers and controllers.

## Security

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/instructure-reaches-ransom-agreement.html) Instructure reached a ransom agreement with ShinyHunters on May 11 — one day before the pay-or-leak deadline — for an undisclosed amount. ShinyHunters confirmed the 3.65 TB Canvas dataset was destroyed; Canvas has been restored. The breach affected data from approximately 275 million students and staff at 9,000 schools.

## Geopolitics

- [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/05/11/world/live-news/iran-war-proposal-trump) Trump called the U.S.-Iran ceasefire on "massive life support" after rejecting Tehran's staggered peace proposal as "stupid" and "unacceptable." The Pentagon put the total cost of the Iran war at $29 billion; negotiations continue through Pakistani mediators.

- [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/5/11/iran-war-live-trump-slams-tehrans-reply-israel-kills-2-medics-in-lebanon) The Russia-Ukraine Victory Day ceasefire expired May 11 with both sides reporting multiple violations.
