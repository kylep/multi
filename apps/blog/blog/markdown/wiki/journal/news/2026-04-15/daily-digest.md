## AI Industry

- [NVIDIA Blog](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/open-models-data-tools-accelerate-ai/) NVIDIA announced the Ising family of open-source models aimed at accelerating quantum computing adoption, released alongside new open data and tools targeting AI deployments across industries.

- [IMF](https://www.imf.org/en/publications/weo/issues/2026/04/14/world-economic-outlook-april-2026) The IMF cut its 2026 global growth forecast to 3.1% (down from January projections), citing surging energy prices driven by the Strait of Hormuz blockade. Global inflation is now projected at 4.4%, up 0.6 percentage points from January. Middle East and Central Asia forecasts were slashed by 2 points to 1.9%.

## AI Tooling

- [9to5Google](https://9to5google.com/2026/04/14/gemini-personal-intelligence-global/) Google began rolling out Personal Intelligence globally to all Gemini language regions (excluding Europe) on April 14–15. The feature connects Gemini to Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Photos, Maps, and other Google apps; it is available first to paid subscribers, with free users to follow.

## Security

- [Tenable](https://www.tenable.com/blog/microsofts-april-2026-patch-tuesday-addresses-163-cves-cve-2026-32201) Microsoft's April 2026 Patch Tuesday patched 163 CVEs — the second-largest monthly batch on record — including one zero-day exploited in the wild: CVE-2026-32201, a SharePoint spoofing flaw allowing unauthenticated network attackers to spoof users. The most critical patch is CVE-2026-33824 (CVSS 9.8), an unauthenticated RCE in Windows IKE v2; administrators should prioritize both immediately.

## Geopolitics

- [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-14/us-iran-seek-more-ceasefire-talks-amid-hormuz-blockade) The US and Iran are arranging a second round of peace talks expected in Islamabad within the next two days, per Trump's statement to the New York Post. The Strait of Hormuz blockade entered its third day — Brent crude remains elevated, with analysts warning of $100/barrel if the standoff persists. Pakistan is mediating.

## Local

- [CBC News](https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/toronto-police-world-cup-plans-9.7163872) Toronto police released their FIFA World Cup 2026 security plan, with fewer than 60 days until the tournament opens. Officers from the RCMP, Durham, York, Peel, and OPP will supplement the Toronto force for match days at BMO Field and surrounding fan zones.
