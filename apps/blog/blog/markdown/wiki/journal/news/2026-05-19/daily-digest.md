## AI Industry

- [Ramp AI Index](https://ramp.com/leading-indicators/ai-index-may-2026) Anthropic surpassed OpenAI in business adoption for the first time, reaching 34.4% of businesses in April, up 3.8 percentage points.

- [Android Central](https://www.androidcentral.com/phones/live/google-i-o-2026-live-blog-android-17-android-xr-glasses-and-all-the-gemini-ai-news) Google I/O 2026 announcements: a new Gemini model, Android 17 branded as a "Gemini Intelligence" platform, Android XR glasses preview, and a new Googlebooks laptop category with Gemini built in from partners including Acer, ASUS, and Lenovo.

## Security

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/) NGINX critical heap buffer overflow CVE-2026-42945 (CVSS 9.2) affecting versions 0.6.27 through 1.30.0 came under active exploitation days after public disclosure. Patches are available.

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/) Microsoft Exchange Server spoofing flaw CVE-2026-42897 (CVSS 8.1), a cross-site scripting bug affecting on-premises deployments, is under active exploitation. Microsoft has issued a fix.
