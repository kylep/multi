## AI Industry

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/04/anthropic-says-claude-code-subscribers-will-need-to-pay-extra-for-openclaw-support/) Anthropic ended the ability for Claude Code subscribers to use their subscription to power OpenClaw and other third-party agent tools as of April 4. Users must now pay via the API or a separate "extra usage" billing tier, with some users facing cost increases of up to 50x over their previous monthly outlay.

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/10/anthropic-temporarily-banned-openclaws-creator-from-accessing-claude/) Anthropic temporarily banned OpenClaw creator Peter Steinberger from accessing Claude entirely on April 10. The ban was later reversed; no reason was given publicly.

- [The Letter Two](https://thelettertwo.com/2026/04/10/ai-economy-microsoft-brings-openclaw-m365-copilot) Microsoft is building a new internal team to integrate the OpenClaw agent framework into Microsoft 365 Copilot, potentially exposing the framework to hundreds of millions of enterprise users.

## AI Tooling

- [Windows Central](https://www.windowscentral.com/microsoft/windows-11/microsoft-finally-begins-removing-copilot-from-notepad-on-windows-11-but-the-ai-still-persists) Microsoft began removing the Copilot branding from Windows 11 apps on April 11 — Notepad's AI writing mode is now labeled "Writing Tools" rather than Copilot. The underlying AI functionality is unchanged; only the branding is being removed.

- [Winbuzzer](https://winbuzzer.com/2026/04/10/google-gemini-now-generates-interactive-visualizations-in-ch-xcxwbn/) Gemini's interactive visualization feature — previously limited to the $200/month Ultra tier — is now rolling out globally to Gemini Pro users. Users can rotate 3D molecules, run physics simulations, and adjust variables via sliders directly in chat.

## Security

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/new-chrome-zero-day-cve-2026-5281-under.html) CVE-2026-39987 (CVSS 9.3): a pre-authentication RCE in the Marimo Python notebook framework was exploited in the wild within 10 hours of public disclosure. The unauthenticated terminal WebSocket endpoint allows full shell access. All versions before 0.23.0 are affected; patch is available.

## Geopolitics

- [ibtimes.com.au](https://www.ibtimes.com.au/strait-hormuz-traffic-remains-near-standstill-april-11-despite-us-iran-ceasefire-iran-limits-1866126) Hormuz shipping remains near standstill three days into the ceasefire — 5 to 20 vessels transited the strait in the past 24 hours against a pre-war daily average of roughly 60. Iran says the US and Israel violated ceasefire terms; IEA called it the most significant oil supply disruption in market history.

- [CBS News](https://www.cbsnews.com/live-updates/iran-trump-ceasefire-strait-hormuz-israel-war-hezbollah-continues/) US-Iran direct talks opened in Islamabad today with Vice President Vance leading the US delegation. Core sticking points are Washington's demand for zero uranium enrichment and full nuclear site dismantlement versus Tehran's demand for de-escalation guarantees.
