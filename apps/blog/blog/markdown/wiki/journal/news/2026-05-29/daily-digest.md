## AI Industry

- [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-05-28/anthropic-raises-at-965-billion-valuation-eclipsing-openai) Anthropic raised $65 billion in a new funding round, valuing the company at $965 billion — overtaking OpenAI's valuation for the first time. The company reported a $47 billion annual revenue run rate, up from $30 billion earlier this year, driven largely by Claude Code adoption.

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/amp/2026/05/28/anthropic-open-ai-startup-value.html) Alongside the funding announcement, Anthropic released Claude Opus 4.8. Claude Managed Agents now support execution in customer-controlled sandboxes connected to private MCP servers.

- [Bloomberg/Google Cloud Blog](https://cloud.google.com/blog/products/ai-machine-learning/innovations-from-google-io-26-on-google-cloud) At Google I/O 2026, Google unveiled Gemini 3.5 Flash — claiming it rivals large flagship models at Flash-tier speeds — and Gemini Omni, a multimodal model capable of generating any output format from any input. Gemini Spark, a 24/7 autonomous agent for Workspace enterprise customers, was also announced.

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/11/openai-eu-cyber-model-anthropic-mythos-gpt.html) OpenAI is preparing to file its confidential IPO prospectus in the coming weeks, targeting a public offering as early as September.

- [NVIDIA Newsroom](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/meta-builds-ai-infrastructure-with-nvidia) NVIDIA reported record Q1 2026 revenue of $81.6 billion driven by AI chip demand. Its multi-year infrastructure deal with Meta — estimated at $35–67 billion — covers Blackwell and next-generation Vera Rubin GPUs plus Grace CPUs.

## Security

- [SecurityWeek](https://www.securityweek.com/new-dirty-frag-linux-vulnerability-possibly-exploited-in-attacks/) A two-flaw chain dubbed "Dirty Frag" (CVE-2026-43284 and CVE-2026-43500) allows unprivileged Linux users to escalate to root. The vulnerabilities are under active investigation for exploitation in the wild.

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/microsoft-releases-mitigation-for.html) A Canvas education platform extortion attack threatens to leak data on 275 million students and faculty from nearly 9,000 institutions. A cybercrime group is demanding ransom after breaching the LMS provider's systems.

- [SecurityWeek](https://www.securityweek.com/cisco-patches-another-sd-wan-zero-day-the-sixth-exploited-in-2026/) Cisco patched a sixth SD-WAN zero-day exploited in 2026, an authentication bypass (CVE-2026-20182) allowing remote admin access via crafted packets. Linked to threat actor UAT-8616.

- [SecurityWeek](https://securityboulevard.com/2026/05/three-cves-and-the-may-2026-exploit-chain-nobodys-taking-seriously/) NGINX Rift (CVE-2026-42945), a heap buffer overflow in ngx_http_rewrite_module present since 2008, came under active exploitation days after public disclosure on May 13.
