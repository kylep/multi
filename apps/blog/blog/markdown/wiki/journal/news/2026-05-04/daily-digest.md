## AI Industry

- [Microsoft Security Blog](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/blog/2026/05/01/microsoft-agent-365-now-generally-available-expands-capabilities-and-integrations/) Microsoft Agent 365 went GA on May 1, bringing Entra network access controls to Copilot Studio agents. The product detects unsanctioned AI use and blocks prompt-injection attacks at the network layer.

- [The Register](https://www.theregister.com/2026/04/28/microsofts_github_shifts_to_metered/) GitHub Copilot is moving from flat-rate to usage-based billing starting June 1. A preview billing dashboard launches in early May ahead of the transition.

- [Voxfor](https://www.voxfor.com/gemini-4-google-next-ai-model-ecosystem/) Google I/O 2026 is confirmed for May 19–20. Gemini 4 is expected to preview at the event.

- [TechStartups](https://techstartups.com/2026/05/01/top-tech-news-today-may-1-2026/) SoftBank is planning to list a new AI and robotics subsidiary in the US at a valuation of up to $100 billion.

## AI Tooling

- [The Register](https://www.theregister.com/2026/04/28/microsofts_github_shifts_to_metered/) GitHub Copilot shifts to metered billing on June 1; flat-rate individual subscriptions are being phased out in favor of per-request pricing for frontier model calls.

## Security

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/cisa-adds-4-exploited-flaws-to-kev-sets.html) CISA added four actively exploited flaws to the KEV catalog with a May 8 federal remediation deadline. The batch includes a CVSS 9.9 flaw in SimpleHelp remote support software and vulnerabilities in Samsung MagicINFO 9 Server and D-Link DIR-823X routers.

## Geopolitics

- [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/05/03/world/live-news/iran-war-news) Trump described US-Iran nuclear talks as "very positive" on May 3. The US Navy began escorting commercial vessels through the Strait of Hormuz as of the same day; Brent crude has eased from last week's peak above $126/barrel.

- [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/) Iranian Nobel Peace laureate Narges Mohammadi was hospitalized after collapsing in Evin Prison. Her legal team has called for her immediate release.
