## AI Industry

- [Fortune](https://fortune.com/) Anthropic has overtaken OpenAI on revenue, running at $47B annualized and projecting profitability in 2026 — a year ahead of schedule. OpenAI is on track for $25-33B annualized. Ramp corporate spend data shows Anthropic passed OpenAI in business subscriptions in May 2026.

- [BigGo Finance](https://finance.biggo.com/news/6f0c6bb2-795f-4c57-9d09-6db691d7638a) Google delayed Gemini 3.5 Pro to July 17, scrapping the existing architecture for a full redesign. The rebuilt model targets a 2M token context window, a Deep Think Reasoning Layer, and cost-competitive pricing against GPT-5.6 and Fable 5.

- [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/technology/ai/articles/meta-compute-launch-sends-ai-015258040.html) Meta launched Meta Compute, a cloud unit selling spare AI infrastructure to outside customers, putting it in direct competition with AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. The announcement sent semiconductor stocks lower as it signaled AI compute scarcity may be easing.

- [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-07/microsoft-replaces-openai-anthropic-with-own-ai-in-some-apps) Microsoft is replacing OpenAI and Anthropic models with its own in-house models in Excel and Outlook to reduce AI costs.

## AI Tooling

- [OpenAI](https://openai.com/) GPT-5.6 is now publicly available. The release includes three tiers: Sol (flagship with Ultra subagent mode and Max reasoning), Terra (GPT-5.5-level quality at half the cost), and Luna (fast tier).

- [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news) Anthropic launched a beta reflection dashboard for Claude that lets users track usage patterns, set quiet hours, and review prompts to use the tool more intentionally.

- [Microsoft Tech Community](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/blog/microsoft365copilotblog/what%e2%80%99s-new-in-microsoft-365-copilot--june-2026/4529572) Anthropic's Claude models are now selectable in Microsoft Copilot Chat for complex analysis, document understanding, and structured content generation.

## Security

- [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/07/sharepoint-rce-cve-2026-45659-added-to.html) CVE-2026-45659, a remote code execution flaw in SharePoint Server (2016/2019/Subscription Edition), is being actively exploited. Any authenticated user with Site Member permissions can trigger it. CISA added it to the Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog and mandated federal agency patching by July 4. Microsoft patches shipped in May 2026.

- [CISA](https://www.cisa.gov/news-events/alerts/2026/07/10/cisa-adds-two-known-exploited-vulnerabilities-catalog) CISA also added CVE-2026-48558, an authentication bypass in SimpleHelp remote support software, with a maximum CVSS score of 10.0.
