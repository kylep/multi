## AI Industry

- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/24/google-agile-robots-ai-robotics.html) Google DeepMind and Agile Robots announced a partnership to integrate Gemini Robotics foundation models into the company's industrial robot fleet. Agile Robots has deployed over 20,000 systems globally across manufacturing, automotive, and logistics.

## AI Tooling

- [OpenAI Help Center](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/6825453-chatgpt-release-notes) ChatGPT removed its legacy deep research mode on March 26; the current deep research experience is unchanged and historical conversations remain accessible across all plan tiers including Enterprise and Edu.

- [OpenAI Help Center](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/6825453-chatgpt-release-notes) OpenAI launched Codex for Students for verified university students in the US and Canada, offering $100 in ChatGPT credits (2,500 credits) claimable at chatgpt.com/codex/students via SheerID verification with a university email. Credits expire 12 months from the grant date.

## Security

- [BleepingComputer](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/microsoft/microsoft-march-2026-patch-tuesday-fixes-2-zero-days-79-flaws/) CVE-2026-26144 (Microsoft Excel): Copilot agent mode can exfiltrate data via unintended network egress, enabling zero-click information disclosure. Fix is included in the March 2026 cumulative update.

- [BleepingComputer](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/microsoft/microsoft-march-2026-patch-tuesday-fixes-2-zero-days-79-flaws/) CVE-2026-26110 and CVE-2026-26113 (CVSS 8.4): Critical RCE vulnerabilities in Microsoft Office exploitable through the preview pane with no user interaction, using type confusion and untrusted pointer dereference. Both patched in the March 2026 Patch Tuesday.

## Geopolitics

- [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Strait_of_Hormuz_crisis) Iran formally rejected the US ceasefire proposal and issued its own counterproposal as of March 25; the Strait of Hormuz remains closed to shipping traffic, entering its fourth week of disruption.

## Local

Whitby and other fast-growing municipalities are seeking hospital funding inclusion.
