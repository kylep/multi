## AI Industry

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/18/anthropic-has-acquired-the-dev-tools-startup-used-by-openai-google-and-cloudflare/) Anthropic acquired Stainless, the New York startup behind automated SDK generation tools used by OpenAI, Google, Cloudflare, and others, for more than $300 million. Going forward the tools will be exclusive to Anthropic; Stainless's hosted SDK generator is being wound down for existing customers, though they retain full rights to SDKs already generated.

## AI Tooling

- [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/personal-finance-chatgpt/) OpenAI launched a personal finance feature in ChatGPT for Pro users in the US, allowing bank account linking via Plaid across 12,000+ financial institutions. The integration provides read-only access to balances, transactions, investments, and liabilities; ChatGPT cannot make transfers or see full account numbers.

- [PCWorld](https://www.pcworld.com/article/3142744/google-just-made-big-changes-to-gemini-usage-limits.html) Google replaced Gemini's fixed daily request caps with a compute-based system that accounts for prompt complexity, features used, and conversation length, refreshing every five hours under a weekly cap. AI Pro subscribers get 4x standard limits; AI Ultra subscribers get 20x.

## Security

- [SecurityWeek](https://www.securityweek.com/grafana-confirms-breach-after-hackers-claim-they-stole-data/) Grafana Labs confirmed that Coinbase Cartel — a group linked to ShinyHunters, Scattered Spider, and Lapsus$ — obtained a GitHub token and used it to download Grafana's codebase. No customer data was compromised. Grafana declined the extortion demand, reset credentials, and initiated forensic review.

- [Security Affairs](https://securityaffairs.com/192204/security/cve-2026-42897-microsoft-confirms-active-exploitation-of-exchange-server-zero-day.html) Microsoft confirmed active exploitation of CVE-2026-42897, a CVSS 8.1 XSS vulnerability in Exchange Server (Subscription Edition, 2016, 2019), and released temporary mitigation measures. A full patch is still pending for older versions.

## Geopolitics

- [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/05/11/world/live-news/iran-war-proposal-trump) President Trump described the Iran-US ceasefire as on "massive life support" as both sides continue firing in the Strait of Hormuz despite the truce. Trump set five new preconditions for resuming nuclear deal talks, including delivery of 400kg of enriched uranium to the US and limiting Iran to one operational nuclear facility.
