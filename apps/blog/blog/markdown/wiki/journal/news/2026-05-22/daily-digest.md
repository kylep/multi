## AI Industry

- [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-05-20/openai-preparing-for-ipo-filing-in-days-or-weeks-wsj-reports) OpenAI is preparing a confidential S-1 filing with the SEC as early as May 22, with Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley as advisors. The company targets a September 2026 public listing at a valuation above $1 trillion and annualized revenue of approximately $25 billion.

- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/20/anthropic-will-pay-xai-1-25-billion-per-month-for-compute/) SpaceX's public S-1 filing reveals that Anthropic is paying xAI $1.25 billion per month for GPU compute access at the Colossus 1 data center in Memphis — approximately 300 MW and 220,000 NVIDIA GPUs — through May 2029, with a 90-day exit clause for either party.

- [Data Center Dynamics](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/spacex-ipo-filing-reveals-anthropic-set-to-pay-musks-firm-125bn-a-month-to-rent-xai-data-center-space/) SpaceX filed its S-1 with the SEC targeting a Nasdaq listing under ticker SPCX as early as June 12, 2026, at a $1.75 trillion valuation. The filing discloses 2025 revenue of $18.7 billion and 10.3 million Starlink subscribers across 164 countries.

- [Build Fast with AI](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-news-today-may-22-2026) Anthropic projects $10.9 billion in Q2 2026 revenue — up from $4.8 billion in Q1 — putting the company on track for its first quarterly operating profit. Enterprise customers spending over $1 million annually doubled to more than 1,000.

## Security

- [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/05/07/us/canvas-hack-strands-college-students-finals-week) ShinyHunters breached Instructure's Canvas LMS in late April, claiming 3.65 TB of data from approximately 275 million users at roughly 9,000 schools. Instructure paid the ransom on May 11 and received a claim that the data was destroyed. No evidence of passwords, dates of birth, or financial data being taken; names, email addresses, and student IDs were confirmed exposed.
